## The Problem with Video Editors

To bleep words in CapCut, iMovie, or Premiere, you have to manually scrub through your video, find each word, cut the audio at the exact moment, import a bleep sound, and position it precisely. Repeat for every word, then export the whole video.

**Time for 10 words: 30-60 minutes**
