## Description

Instantly censor any word or phrase from your videos and audio files. Whether you're making content ad-friendly, protecting someone's identity, or just having fun - Bleep That makes it easy.

**HOW IT WORKS**

1. Upload your video or audio file
2. AI transcription detects every spoken word
3. Tap the words you want to censor
4. Choose your bleep sound and volume
5. Download your censored video

**KEY FEATURES**

• AI-Powered Transcription - Automatically detects and timestamps every word using Whisper AI
• Interactive Word Selection - Simply tap any word in the transcript to mark it for censoring
• Multiple Bleep Sounds - Choose from classic bleep, silence, or other sound effects
• Adjustable Volume - Fine-tune the bleep volume and original audio ducking
• Quick Word Lists - Instantly censor common profanity with one tap
• Pattern Matching - Find and censor variations of words automatically

**100% PRIVATE**

Your videos never leave your device. All transcription and processing happens locally in the app - no uploads, no cloud processing, no data collection. Your content stays yours.

**PERFECT FOR**

• Content creators making videos YouTube/TikTok-friendly
• Podcasters cleaning up interviews
• Parents editing home videos
• Anyone who needs quick audio censoring

**SUPPORTED FORMATS**

• Video: MP4, MOV, WebM
• Audio: MP3, WAV, M4A
• Files up to 10 minutes
